Treasury emission

Treasury emission is dynamic — it scales with average block time and wallet load on a faucet-aligned baseline (up to 1 PERC per 7-minute window at 1.0×). Manual sends from evolve_treasury are disabled after launch; emission, regeneration, and faucet payouts continue.

Network nodes

Chronoflux five-point pentagon — every registered wallet by current block height (highest of scenario, chain, or relay). Five rings mark 0%–100% of network max. Wallets appear for 7 minutes after their last heartbeat, then drop off.
